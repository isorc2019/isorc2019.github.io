REGISTRATION

Author registration deadline: April 6th, 2019
Early registration deadline: April 14th, 2019
Late registration deadline: April 24th, 2019

Registration link here.

Each paper (either full or poster/demo paper) requires a full registration (640 USD for IEEE members and 770 USD for IEEE non-members). Authors must indicate their EasyChair paper ID and title in the registration process. Papers with extra pages, must select the appropriate number of pages in the registration process and the appropriate additional costs will be added to the fees (100 USD per page).

Registration prices are listed below:

EARLY REGISTRATION (until April 14th)
- Full (IEEE member): 640 USD
- Full (IEEE non-member): 770 USD
- Student (IEEE member): 420 USD
- Student (IEEE non-member): 510 USD

LATE REGISTRATION (from April 15th until April 24th)
- Full (IEEE member): 770 USD
- Full (IEEE non-member): 925 USD
- Student (IEEE member): 530 USD
- Student (IEEE non-member): 640 USD

On site fees can be found in the registration link.

Extra banquet tickets ( 70 USD each ) can be bought also through the registration site.

One day registration is posible (400 USD), but it excludes the banquet which can be additionally purchased.
